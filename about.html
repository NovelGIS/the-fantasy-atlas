About

✦ About The Fantasy Atlas

The Fantasy Atlas is an interactive mapping companion designed to bring fictional worlds to life.

Built as a fusion of storytelling and geospatial design, it lets readers explore characters, locations, and events as they move through a story—visually and spatially. Instead of imagining a world only on the page, you can see it unfold on a map.

Whether you're tracking character journeys, exploring key locations, or following along chapter by chapter, The Fantasy Atlas turns reading into an experience you can navigate.

✦ What You Can Explore

Interactive story maps tied to chapters and events
Character movement and positioning across time and space
Locations that evolve as the story progresses
Book-specific layers that let you filter and focus your view
A reading experience that blends narrative + geography

✦ Why It Exists

Stories often live in our imagination as places we can almost see—but rarely navigate.

The Fantasy Atlas was created to bridge that gap:
to take the invisible geography of fiction and make it explorable, intuitive, and alive.

It's built for readers who think in maps, and map-lovers who think in stories.

✦ Built By

Created by Novel GIS, a geospatial developer focused on combining interactive mapping with storytelling systems. This project blends GIS tools, web mapping, and narrative design to create a new way of experiencing books.

✦ Notes

The Fantasy Atlas is an independent fan project and is not affiliated with the original authors or publishers of any featured works.
