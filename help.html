Help

✦ Overview

This interactive map tracks character locations and movements throughout fantasy book series. Use the controls to explore by series, book and chapter.

✦ Series Selector

Choose a series from the drop-down to filter the map to the first book and chapter of the series.
Switching series resets the book selection.

✦ Book Selector

Choose a book from the drop-down to filter the map to that story.
Switching books resets the chapter selection.

✦ Chapter Selector

Select a chapter to view character positions at that point in the story.

  • Characters update dynamically as chapters change
  • Chapter range depends on selected book

✦ Chapter Navigation Buttons

Use the arrow buttons to move through chapters one at a time.

  • ◀ Previous Chapter
  • ▶ Next Chapter

Buttons will gray out when you reach the first or last chapter of a book.

✦ Labels Toggle

Turn character labels on or off.

  • On: Character names are visible
  • Off: Character names are hidden

✦ Characters on the Map

Each marker represents a character or multiple characters at that location at the start of the chapter.
Some characters only appear in certain books or chapters.

✦ Notes

  • If a button is disabled (greyed out), you've reached the start or end of available chapters
  • Map updates may take a moment when switching between books
